About me

My name is Cyrus, and I was born in 2004. Usually, I fill my spare time with reading books, writing short stories, playing video games, doing nothing, and riding around on my bike. I also have a small drone that I sometimes fly in the park during spring/summer/autumn.

Nowadays, I am learning about Computers and electronics at Kuben VGS. Our subjects are Social studies, Norwegain, Computer-and informationtechnology, and electronics. At the moment, we have a project where we are making our own HTML website about ourselves. Additionally, we are learning the basics of Arduino by programming electronic circuits that we have made.

Additionally, we have started our own business where we find interesting Arduino projects for secondary school students. We buy the components required, put together an educational kit, and teach the students how the circuitry and programming works.
